TEEN GIRLS IN
BLOCKCHAIN

Teen Girls in Blockchain is an initiative aimed at encouraging and inspiring young women to explore the field of blockchain technology and its potential for creating positive impact in various industries. The goal of this initiative is to encourage and empower young women to pursue careers in technology and to close the gender gap in the industry by increasing the representation of women in the field of blockchain. This can include education, mentorship, and networking opportunities, among other initiatives.
