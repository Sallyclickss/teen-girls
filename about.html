Founded in 2008 and established in 2012, Teen Girls in Blockchain is an initiative aimed at encouraging and inspiring young women to explore the field of blockchain technology and its potential for creating positive impact in various industries. The goal of this initiative is to encourage and empower young women to pursue careers in technology and to close the gender gap in the industry by increasing the representation of women in the field of blockchain. This can include education, mentorship, and networking opportunities, among other initiatives.Our community is represented by all abilities, regardless of gender, race, ethnicity, class, age, or sexual orientation.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a supportive community for teenage girls interested in blockchain technology and its applications.
TGIB Founder, Nkene Nzeala, TGIBcom 2024
